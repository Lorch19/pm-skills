# User Research

Plan, execute, and synthesize user research studies.

## Research Methods

| Method | Best For | Sample Size | Time |
|--------|----------|-------------|------|
| User interviews | Deep understanding of needs | 5-8 | 2-4 weeks |
| Usability testing | Evaluating a design/flow | 5-8 | 1-2 weeks |
| Surveys | Quantifying attitudes | 100+ | 1-2 weeks |
| Card sorting | Information architecture | 15-30 | 1 week |
| Diary studies | Behavior over time | 10-15 | 2-8 weeks |
| A/B testing | Comparing design choices | Statistical sig. | 1-4 weeks |

## Interview Guide Structure

1. **Warm-up** (5 min): Build rapport
2. **Context** (10 min): Current workflow
3. **Deep dive** (20 min): Specific topic
4. **Reaction** (10 min): Concepts or prototypes
5. **Wrap-up** (5 min): Anything missed

## Analysis Framework

- **Affinity mapping**: Group observations into themes
- **Impact/effort matrix**: Prioritize findings
- **Journey mapping**: Visualize experience over time
- **Jobs to be done**: What users are hiring your product to do

## Deliverables

Research plan, interview guide, synthesis report, highlight reel.

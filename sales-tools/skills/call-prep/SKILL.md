# Call Prep

Get fully prepared for any sales call in minutes.

## What I Do

1. **Gather Context** — From CRM/email/chat or your input
2. **Research Supplement** — Web search for recent company news
3. **Synthesize** — Account snapshot, attendee profiles, agenda, questions

## Output

```markdown
## Call Prep: [Company] — [Meeting Type]

### Account Snapshot
[Company overview, recent activity, deal stage]

### Attendees
| Name | Role | Key Talking Points |
|------|------|--------------------|

### Context & History
[Prior interactions, open items]

### Suggested Agenda
1. [Topic] — [Time]

### Discovery Questions
- [Question targeting specific pain point]

### Potential Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|

### Internal Notes
[Strategy, goals for this call]
```

## Adapts by Meeting Type

- **Discovery**: Focus on pain points, qualification questions
- **Demo**: Tailor to attendee roles, prepare objection handling
- **Negotiation**: Competitive positioning, value reinforcement
- **QBR**: Usage data, success metrics, expansion opportunities

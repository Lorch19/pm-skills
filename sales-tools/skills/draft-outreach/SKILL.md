# Draft Outreach

Research first, then draft personalized outreach.

## Execution Flow

1. **Parse Request** — Who to reach, context
2. **Research First** — Always research before drafting
3. **Identify Hook** — Priority: trigger event > mutual connection > their content > company initiative > role-based pain
4. **Draft Message** — AIDA structure (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
5. **Create Draft** — Email, LinkedIn, or plain text

## Templates

- **Cold Outreach** — First touch with no prior relationship
- **Warm Outreach** — Referral or shared connection
- **Re-Engagement** — Reviving a cold conversation
- **Post-Event** — Following up from a conference/webinar

## Follow-Up Sequence

- Day 3: Different angle, same thread
- Day 7: Value-add (relevant content/insight)
- Day 14: Break-up email (last attempt)

## Email Guidelines

- Plain text only, no markdown formatting
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- One clear CTA
- Subject line under 50 characters

# Competitive Intelligence

Research competitors and produce interactive battlecards.

## Execution Flow

1. **Gather Seller Context** — Your company, product, positioning
2. **Research Your Company** — Strengths, differentiators
3. **Research Each Competitor** — 8 searches per competitor
4. **Build Output** — Comparison matrix + per-competitor cards

## Output Per Competitor

- **Profile**: Overview, positioning, target market
- **Recent Releases**: Product updates, announcements
- **Differentiators**: Where they win vs. where you win
- **Pricing**: Published pricing, packaging differences
- **Talk Tracks**: How to position against them
- **Objection Handling**: Common objections + responses
- **Landmine Questions**: Questions that expose their weaknesses

# Example: Proactive Research

**Omri:** "I'm working on the Q2 roadmap — thinking about prioritizing NHI lifecycle management."

**Advisor response:**

Good instinct — NHI is Linx's differentiator. But before we roadmap it, I should check what competitors shipped recently. ConductorOne announced NHI governance in Feb 2025 and Zluri launched their Identity Security Platform in March 2026.

**Want me to search for:**
- What ConductorOne's NHI governance actually includes (vs. announcement)?
- Whether Lumos has added NHI coverage since the Gartner report?
- Any new NHI-focused startups that entered the market?

This would take ~5 minutes and could change the priority.

---

*[After Omri says yes]*

**Research results (5 bullets):**

1. **ConductorOne NHI:** Announced service account discovery + access reviews for NHI. No autonomous remediation for NHI. No agentic identity coverage. Still early — mostly discovery, not lifecycle.

2. **Lumos:** No new NHI announcements since Gartner report. Still focused on human SaaS governance. Identity Security Agents (March 2026) are about human identity automation, not NHI.

3. **New entrant: Astrix Security** raised $45M for NHI security. Focus on service accounts, API keys, OAuth tokens. Discovery + posture only, no governance lifecycle. Could become a partner or competitor.

4. **Market signal:** RSA 2026 had 12 vendors pitching NHI governance — up from 3 at RSA 2025. Category is exploding. Window to lead is narrowing.

5. **Gartner says:** "Most IGA vendor solutions only govern service accounts right now." Full NHI lifecycle (beyond service accounts to certificates, API keys, AI agents) is still wide open.

**My take:** NHI lifecycle is the right priority IF you scope it correctly. Don't build "NHI access reviews" (that's what ConductorOne and Astrix are doing). Build "NHI lifecycle with autonomous remediation" — that's the gap no one fills and it reinforces the "only we" positioning.

**Series B angle:** "Only vendor with full NHI lifecycle management + autonomous remediation" is a strong differentiator for investor narrative.

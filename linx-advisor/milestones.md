## Pre-Onboarding (March 29 — April 11)
**Theme:** Arrive prepared. Test the system. Build the muscle before day 1.
- [ ] Test daily log habit for 2 weeks (log something 8+ out of 14 days)
- [ ] Read and internalize competitive matrix — be able to name all 8 competitors cold
- [ ] Read all 4 Linx onboarding PDFs
- [ ] Prep "first week questions" list for Niv and Israel

- [ ] Validate system: what worked, what to adjust before day 1

## Phase 1: Learn & Map (Days 1-30) — Due May 12, 2026
**Theme:** Understand the terrain. Earn the right to have opinions.

- [ ] 5+ customer conversations (direct or observed) — count: 0
- [ ] Stakeholder map populated: Niv style observed + 2 pushbacks documented; Israel priorities confirmed; eng lead names + working styles known
- [ ] Product architecture: can sketch the Linx system diagram unprompted

**Theme:** Be the person the team turns to.

- [ ] Owns a recognized product domain (governance, compliance, or NHI)

# Mission: Phase 2 — Contribute & Validate (Days 31-60)

## Goal
Ship real work. Build credibility through artifacts, not opinions. Earn a seat at the strategy table.

## Timeline
- Start: May 12, 2026
- End: June 11, 2026

## Success Criteria
- [ ] First PRD or product spec written and reviewed by Niv
- [ ] 10+ cumulative customer conversations (3+ Omri-led)
- [ ] 1 data-backed roadmap recommendation presented to leadership
- [ ] Battle cards shared with GTM, feedback from 1+ sales rep
- [ ] Trust signal: Niv delegates a product decision to Omri
- [ ] 5+ decisions logged with reasoning in decision journal

## Key Activities

### Weeks 5-6: Ship the Spec
- Write first PRD for highest-priority governance feature (likely from V1.1 backlog).
- Use `prd-development` skill + Linx layer (competitive check, CISO credibility, Series B angle).
- Get Niv's review. Iterate. Ship.

### Weeks 7-8: Data-Backed Recommendations
- Synthesize customer conversations into patterns. What are the top 3 asks?
- Cross-reference with competitive landscape: where do customer needs align with competitive gaps?
- Present 1 roadmap recommendation to leadership with evidence: customer data + competitive context + Series B implication.
- Share updated battle cards with sales. Get real-deal feedback.

## Anti-Patterns
- Shipping artifacts nobody asked for
- Avoiding hard conversations with Niv or engineering
- Over-indexing on competitive analysis vs. customer evidence
- Perfectionism on PRDs — ship fast, iterate

## Skills to Use
- `prd-development` — PRD writing with Linx review layer
- `metrics-design` — define success criteria for shipped features
- `/pm:strategy` — strategy sessions with competitive stress-test
- `roadmap-planning` — roadmap recommendation preparation

# Mission: Platform Strategy & Series B Foundation

## Goal
Define the platform expansion sequence and build the data foundation for Series B. This runs in parallel with Phases 2-3 — it's the strategic thinking layer on top of the daily execution.

## Timeline
- Start: May 2026 (after Phase 1 customer/market understanding)
- Ongoing through Series B prep

## Workstreams

### A. Market Segmentation
- Map IGA buyer segments: verticals, company sizes, use cases, buying triggers
- Identify Linx's best-fit segment (likely: cloud-forward enterprises, 1K-10K employees, NHI-heavy)
- Validate with customer data from Phase 1-2

### B. Platform Expansion Sequence
- Which capabilities to build next, in what order, for which segments?
- The question: governance depth first (close the 2.5/5 gap) or double down on AI/agentic (widen the wedge)?
- Stress-test: is "one-stop-shop for identity security" achievable at Series A scale? What's the minimum viable platform?

### C. TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis
- Full market sizing using `tam-sam-som-calculator`
- Not just NHI — the whole identity security platform play
- Frame for investor consumption

### D. Metrics Framework
- Design the metrics that prove platform trajectory to investors
- ARR growth, NRR, customer diversification, module adoption, land-and-expand
- Use `metrics-design` skill + Series B benchmarks

### E. Series B Narrative
- Combine everything: market size + competitive positioning + platform trajectory + customer traction + metrics
- Collaborate with Israel on the pitch

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Market segmentation validated with customer + sales data
- [ ] Platform expansion roadmap with sequenced capabilities
- [ ] TAM/SAM/SOM analysis complete
- [ ] Metrics framework designed and instrumented
- [ ] Contribution to Series B materials
